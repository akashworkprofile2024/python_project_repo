Project Banking System:

1. Register Account
   a. A/C holder Name
   b. phone number
   c. Address
   d. Email
   e. Account Type Saving or Current (Both Random generated unique 12 digit)
   f. 4 Digit pin generate
   g. Active and Inactive Status
   h. Back to login after register

2. Access Account using A/C number and Pin
   a. Withdraw
   b. Deposit
   c. Balance Check
   d. Account Close Request
   e. Account update Request name , phone , address , email
   f. Exit

3. Bank System Admin Panel

   a. Request Notification
   b. All Client Details
   c. Update Account operation
   d. Close Account
   e. Exit
